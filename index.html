Where craftsmanship meets conscience.

WHAT WE DO

End-to-End Production Management

We lift the operational burden off your design team. From the initial fiber specification to customs clearance, we own the supply chain execution.

Hover over a service card to explore details

01 / Material & Fabric Sourcing

We source raw yarns, develop custom knits and weaves, and supply low-impact dyed textiles. We verify all GOTS, GRS, and FSC certifications at the source, ensuring raw materials align with your sustainability targets.

  • Custom textile development
  • Sustainable fiber sourcing (Organic, Recycled, Smart-cell)
  • Certification vetting & verification mapping

02 / Custom Manufacturing & Sampling

Our local teams interpret your tech packs, create precise samples, manage fit grading, and fabricate photo-shoot samples. We optimize pattern layouts to reduce cut waste before starting bulk runs.

  • Proto sample & SMS creation
  • Size grading & digital pattern files
  • Yield optimization & marker planning

03 / Rigid Quality Assurance

We deploy inspectors directly to factory floors. We carry out fabric checks, inline stitching reviews, and final pre-shipment inspections following AQL 2.5 standards to guarantee zero-defect shipments.

  • AQL 2.5 inline & final inspections
  • Color matching verification (Spectrophotometer audits)
  • Shrinkage, twist, and seam strength testing

04 / Global Logistics & Customs

We handle export paperwork, consolidate shipments, coordinate ocean/air shipping, and manage customs clearance, delivering direct to your distribution centers on a landed basis (DDP).

  • Landed price modeling (DDP, FOB, CIF)
  • HS Code classification & duty optimization
  • Real-time shipment tracking
